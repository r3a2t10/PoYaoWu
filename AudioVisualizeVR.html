So That I Can Forget You

A VR audio visualizer translating music into immersive motion and spatial emotion.

Unity

C#

Virtual Reality

Audio Spectrum Analysis

Visual Design

TIME | 2018
AUTHORS | Po-Yao Wu
MOTIVATION |

So That I Can Forget You is a VR-based audio visualizer built around the song of the same name by Astro Bunny. The project analyzes the track’s spectral and rhythmic structures and transforms them into motion, rotation, and spatial choreography within a 360° virtual environment. Visual elements are derived from the song’s lyrical imagery, guiding the viewer into a space where sound and metaphor intertwine. The work invites viewers to feel the song—to inhabit its emotional landscape, dissolve into its atmosphere, and encounter its beauty through an immersive, synesthetic VR form.
